about

 

andrea nasuto was born in gargano, italy.

in 2014, he co-directed made of limestone, a documentary about leaving or staying in the place where he was born, featured by university of cambridge and atlantic media. he graduated in quantitative finance.
he has worked in design research, data science, architecture, web development.

he skates in bedstuy, new york. marco is his brother.

marco nasuto was born in gargano, italy.

in 2014, he co-directed Made of Limestone, a documentary about leaving or staying in the place where he was born, featured by University of Cambridge and Atlantic Media.
he graduated in aerospace engineering. he has worked in growth hacking and aerospace engineering. he bikes in copenaghen.
